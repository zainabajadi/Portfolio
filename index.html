About Me

I'm a college student currently pursuing a degree in computer information systems. Being influenced by technology in my everyday life, I want to grow with tech and advance my knowledge in the industry. The benefits, efficiency, and profit of being well-rounded in technology are opportunities to take advantage of. Using my passion for Art, I often channel it into tech, with the unlimited resources available to me digitally. Expressing myself and being creative with my thinking is who I am at the core. Not only do I want to influence others with my artistry, but I also want to benefit others with the skills that I have.
